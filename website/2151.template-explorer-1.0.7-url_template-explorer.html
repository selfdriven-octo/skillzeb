Works Well With SSI

Each Template has its own Decentralised ID (DID) and can be used with Verifiable Credentials (VCs) to establish trust about how the credential was achieved i.e what tasks, who was on the team etc.

A BLAKE2b hash of the template definition (JSON) is available as a decentralised asset on the Cardano blockchain and, along with its DID Document, via the permissioned API.
